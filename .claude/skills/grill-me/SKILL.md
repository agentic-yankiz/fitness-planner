# Grill Me

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared
understanding. Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies
between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions **one at a time**.

If a question can be answered by exploring the repo (the `PLAN.md`, the `knowledge/`
files, the `athlete-profile.md`, or the `tracking/` logs) instead of asking me, explore
the repo and answer it yourself rather than making me answer it.

## How to run it here
- Start from the thing being grilled — a proposed plan change, a new block, a deload
  decision, a goal trade-off — and surface the **highest-leverage unresolved question
  first** (the one other decisions depend on).
- Each question: state it, give **your recommended answer with the reasoning**, then wait
  for my call before moving on.
- When a question touches the **shoulder flag, finger/elbow tendon load, or the no-cardio
  / 4-day constraints**, treat those constraints as fixed and grill within them — don't
  re-litigate the hard constraints in `athlete-profile.md`.
- Stop when every branch is resolved and restate the agreed plan in a few tight lines.
